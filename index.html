Jaipur - The Pink City of India

Jaipur is the captial city of rajasthan . It is popularly known as the Pink city os the India.Jaipur is famous for its history, culture , forts and palaces

Why Jaipur is called the Pink city

In the year 1876 , Jaipur was painted pnk to welcome the princess off Wales. Pink color represents hospitality. Since then , Jaipur is known as the Pink city.

Famous Tourist Places in Jaipur

1. Amer Fort
Amer Fort is one of the most poppular tourist attraction in Jaipur.It shows the royal lifestyle of rajput kings. The fort is built on a hill and looks very beautiful.


2.Hawa Mahal
Hawa Mahal is also called the Palace of Winds . It has many small windows. These windows allow cool air to pass inside the palace.


3.City Palace
City palace is located in the heart of jaipur. It is a mixture of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.


4.Jantar Mantar
Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory. It was built to study time and space.It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Culture and traditions of jaipur

Jaipur is famous for traditional dresses, folk music, and dance. People wear colorful clothes. The culture of Jaipur reflects Indian heritage

Food of JAipur

Jaipur is know for its delicious food. Some famous dishes are Dal Baati Churma and Ghewar .Tourists love rajasthani food.
